# 🛠 Grinding Toolkit

For the really tough grinds (500 000 tiles, 15 000 collectibles, 10 000 turns, 5 000 sells, 500 portals) I've put together a small toolkit that automates the repetitive stuff.

It comes with a standalone app (no Python needed, just double-click the .exe) and also the raw Python scripts if you prefer tweaking them yourself.

Download it here: https://github.com/bidi-codes/hosf-grinding-toolkit

⚠️ All coordinates in the app/scripts are based on my screen resolution. You'll need to find your own using the Mouse Capture tool. It takes 2 minutes, don't skip this step.

💡 Before grinding, I highly recommend quadrupling hero movement speed through the registry. It cuts grind time dramatically. See the Regedit Setup section for how to do it.

---

## 🖱 Mouse Capture

Captures screen coordinates wherever you click. You'll need these for every other tool.

How to use:
1. Start the tool, switch to the game
2. Click on the position you need (a tile, a button, whatever)
3. The coordinates appear in the list
4. Press ESC when done

Use these coordinates in the other tools. Every screen/resolution is different, so this step is essential.

## 🔄 Auto End Turn
For: One more turn (end turn 10 000 times)

Presses E (end turn), then Enter twice (cycle confirmation + player turn confirmation). Runs forever until you press ESC.

Setup:
1. Open the map editor, place only yourself, no enemies
2. Set victory condition to "hold base for 10 001 turns" so you can track progress
3. Disable the "actions still available" popup (the eye icon top-left)
4. Start the tool, switch to game

💡 The default delays work fine. If turns are getting skipped, increase "After cycle confirm" a bit.

## 🚶 Move
For: I will walk 500 000 tiles (cumulative)

Moves your commander back and forth between two points endlessly, restoring movement after each trip.

Setup:
1. Open the map editor, place your commander alone on a flat map
2. Max out Exploration attribute, add Explorer + Globetrotter skills
3. Useful artifacts: Fuel Additives, Novicomp, Red Radiant, Recon Drone
4. Use Mouse Capture to find your two click points (left and right of commander) and the testtools refill movement button
5. Start the tool, switch to game

⚠️ If the commander keeps drifting to one side, adjust the coordinates:
- Drifting right → decrease Point A x
- Drifting left → increase Point B x

💡 With quadrupled speed (regedit trick), set Move Time to 1. With normal speed, 2.25 works.

## 📦 Collect
For: Hoarder (pick up 15 000 equipment) and Yard Sale (sell 5 000 items)

Traverses the map in a snake pattern, collecting items tile by tile. Goes left on even rows, right on odd rows, one tile up between rows.

Setup:
1. Open the map editor, fill a 40x40 area with collectibles (use the duplicate zone to speed this up), or download the pre-made Hoarder workshop map
2. Place your commander in the bottom-left corner
3. Disable "show picked up item info in a dialog" in settings (saves a LOT of time)
4. Use Mouse Capture to find: one tile left, one tile right, one tile up, and the restore button
5. Start the tool, switch to game

💡 Each run collects around 1 000+ items. You'll need about 15 sessions. I did it in batches, took about 2 hours total. Think of it as therapy clicking :D

💡 When you've cleared a map, visit the Black Market at the top and use the Sell tool to sell everything. You can also save in front of the market, sell, reload, sell again until you hit 5 000.

⚠️ If clicking on commander instead of the tile, adjust:
- Left tile → increase x
- Right tile → decrease x

## 💰 Sell
For: Yard Sale (sell 5 000 items in markets)

Clicks the Sell button repeatedly. That's it :D

Setup:
1. Visit a Black Market or Traveling Merchant with items in your inventory
2. Use Mouse Capture to find the Sell button position
3. Start the tool, switch to game

💡 Neat trick: gather 500-1 000 items, save in front of the market, sell everything with the tool, reload, sell again. Repeat until you hit 5 000.

---

## 🌀 Town Portal
For: Instant visit (use town portal 500 times)

Presses G (teleport beam), clicks the first town in the list, clicks Travel. Loops forever.

Setup:
1. You need at least 2 towns
2. Stay inside one of the towns
3. Make sure you have enough aura coins for teleport costs
4. Use Mouse Capture to find: the first town in the teleport list and the Travel button
5. Start the tool, switch to game

💡 You never have to leave town. The loop is G + 2 clicks, takes about 0.7 seconds per teleport. Done in under 6 minutes.

All tools stop with ESC. All tools have a 5-second delay before starting so you can switch to the game window.
